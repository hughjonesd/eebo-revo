<section type="Hard words">
<heading>Sicknes.</heading>
NOw you haue ended your Table, with the names of compoun&#173;
des.  There are certain wordes, very harde for me to vnderstand
as when you doe name <term lang="la">Apophlegmatismus</term>, <term lang="la">Dropax</term>, <term lang="la">Linement</term>. &#38;c.  I knowe
not what thei doe meane, by their proper names, I praie you tell me
the significacions.
