The French Schoole&shy;
maister, wherin is most plain&shy;
lie shewed, the true and most
perfect way of pronouncinge of the
Frenche tongue, without any helpe
of Maister or teacher: set foorthe for
the furtherance of all those whiche
doo studie priuatly in their owne 
study or houses: Vnto the which
is annexed a Vocabularie for al such
woordes as bee vsed in com&shy;
common talkes: by M. Clau&shy;
dius Hollybande, pro&shy;
fessor of the Latin,
Frenche,
And Englishe 
tongues.

<term lang="la">Dum spiro, spero.</term>

&para; Imprinted at London, by
William How: for
Abraham Veale.
1573.
