LATHAMS 
FALCONRY 
OR 
The Faulcons Lure, and Cure: 
IN TWO BOOKES.

The First, concerning the ordering and training vp of all 
Hawkes in generall; especially the HAGGARD 
FAVLCON GENTLE.

The second, teaching approued medicines for the cure 
of all Diseases in them.

Gathered by long practice and experience, and published 
for the delight of noble mindes, and instruction of 
young Faulconers in things pertaining 
to this Princely Art.

By SYMON LATHAM. Gent.

Printed at London for Roger Iackson, and are to be sold at his shop 
neere Fleet-street Conduit, 1614.
</page>
</section>

<wordentry type="h"><form lang="en" lexeme="bathing(n)">BAthing</form> <xpln lang="en">is when you set your Hawke to the water, to wash

away, or get liberty.</xpln></wordentry>

<wordentry type="h"><form lang="en" lexeme="bousing(n)">Bousing</form> <xpln lang="en">is when a Hawke drinketh often, and seemes to be

</wordgroup1>

i first lured.</xpln></wordentry>

<wordentry type="h"><form lang="en" lexeme="lice(n)">Lice,</form> <xpln lang="en">are a small kind of white vermin, running amongst
