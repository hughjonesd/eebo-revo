&#182; A note to vnderstand 
the meaning of newe
deuised Termes.
</heading>

pounded terme: then turne to that place
in our Booke, which the two numbers
do poynt foorth, that are set vpon the word:
And if thou be a Latiniste, take also that
helpe, whyche the Latine terme dothe
yelde.
