The first booke of 
Cattell: 
Wherein is shewed the gouernment of
<i>Oxen, Kine, Calues, and how to vse Bulles and</i>
other cattell to the yoake, and fell. With diuers ap&shy;
proued remedies, to helpe most diseases among cat&shy;
tell: most necessarie for all, especially for hus&shy;
band men, hauing the gouernment of 
any such cattell.

Gathered and set forth by Leonard Mascall.

<lemeformat>
Thou Heardman, keeper of thy beastes,
    when any beast is sickly:
Search herein, and thou shalt find,
    of proued remedies quickly,
</lemeformat>

London
Printed by Iohn Wolfe,
1587.
